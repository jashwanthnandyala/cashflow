Here's a brief description of how to use this web app: Login: Enter the username and PIN for one of the three profiles (Sheldon Cooper, Leonard Hofstadter, or Barry Allen) and click the "Log In" button. Upon successful login, you will see a welcome message and the current date and time. Account Overview: After logging in, you'll see the account's balance, a list of recent transactions, and a summary of the total income, total withdrawals, and earned interest.
View Transactions: The list of recent transactions includes the type (deposit or withdrawal), date, and transaction amount. Transactions are sorted by date.
You can click the "Sort" button to toggle between sorting transactions by date in ascending or descending order. Make a Transfer: To transfer money to another account, click the "Transfer" button. Enter the recipient's username and the amount you want to transfer. If the recipient exists and you have enough funds, the transfer will be completed.
Request a Loan: You can request a loan by clicking the "Request Loan" button. Enter the loan amount you want to borrow. If you have sufficient transaction history and meet the criteria, the loan will be approved and added to your account. Logout: The app has an automatic logout feature that logs you out after 5 minutes of inactivity. You can see the countdown timer in the top right corner.
Sort Transactions: You can sort your transactions by clicking the "Sort" button. This will toggle between sorting transactions by date in ascending or descending order.
